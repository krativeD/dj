About DOJO

DOJO was founded by Owen Matlou and Mahlatsi Mosima with a vision to create a fitness community that empowers individuals to reach their full potential.

Our gym is more than just a place to work out - it's a sanctuary where discipline meets determination, and where every member becomes part of our family.

At DOJO, we believe in a holistic approach to fitness that combines physical training with mental resilience, helping you build not just a stronger body, but a stronger mind.

Our facility at 539 Gompou Street, Rabie Ridge is equipped with top-notch equipment and staffed by passionate professionals dedicated to your success.
